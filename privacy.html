SECTION 01
Introduction

Welcome to Classify ("we", "our", or "us"). Classify is an online platform that helps students discover, compare, and apply to coaching institutes across India for competitive exams like JEE, NEET, NDA, CUET, UPSC and more.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share and protect information about you when you use our website at classify.in and related services (collectively, the "Platform").

By accessing or using Classify, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Platform.

📌 Key Principle: Classify is completely free for students. We earn from coaching institutes — not from selling your personal data. Your data is only used to improve your experience and connect you with the right coaching.
📊
SECTION 02
Data We Collect

2.1 — Information You Provide

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect:

  • Full name, email address and phone number
  • Date of birth and gender
  • City and state of residence
  • Target exam (JEE, NEET, NDA etc.) and target year
  • Current class / education details
  • Application form data when you apply to an institute
  • Reviews and ratings you submit for institutes
  • Messages you send us through the contact form

2.2 — Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the Platform, we automatically collect:

  • IP address and device information (browser, OS)
  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • Search queries you enter on Classify
  • Institutes you view, save or apply to
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2.3 — Information from Institutes

If you are a coaching institute registering on Classify, we collect:

  • Institute name, address and contact details
  • Registration certificates and legal documents
  • Course details, fees and faculty information
  • Bank account / payment details for commission settlements
📌 Note: We only collect information that is necessary to provide our services. We do not collect sensitive data like caste, religion, financial details (except for institutes) or Aadhaar numbers.

SECTION 04
Data Sharing & Disclosure

4.1 — With Coaching Institutes

When you submit an application through Classify, we share your application details with the specific institute you applied to. This is the core service we provide. The institute receives:

  • Your name, phone number and email address
  • Your exam preference and target year
  • Your course preference and message

We do not share your data with institutes you have not applied to.

4.2 — With Service Providers

We may share data with trusted third-party providers who help us operate the platform:

  • Cloud storage and hosting providers (e.g. AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Email and SMS service providers for notifications
  • Analytics providers to understand platform usage
  • Payment processors for institute subscription payments

4.3 — Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data if required by law, court order, or government authority.

❌ We will NEVER: Sell your personal data to advertisers, data brokers or any third party for commercial gain. Your data is not a product.

SECTION 06
Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We implement the following measures:

  • All data is transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS)
  • Passwords are stored using bcrypt hashing — never in plain text
  • Our servers are hosted on ISO-certified cloud infrastructure
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorised staff only
  • Automatic logout after periods of inactivity
⚠️ Important: While we take every precaution, no system is 100% secure. If you suspect your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at security@classify.in
⚖️
SECTION 07
Your Rights

As a user of Classify, you have the following rights over your personal data:

  • Right to Access — You can request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction — You can update incorrect or outdated information
  • Right to Deletion — You can request deletion of your account and data
  • Right to Portability — You can request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — You can opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Right to Object — You can object to how we process your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@classify.in. We will respond within 30 days.

✅ How to delete your account: Log in → Go to Account Settings → Click "Delete My Account". All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
👶
SECTION 08
Children's Privacy

Classify is designed for students preparing for competitive exams. Many of our users may be under 18 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 years of age. If you are under 13, please do not use Classify without a parent or guardian.

If a parent or guardian believes their child under 13 has provided personal data to Classify, please contact us at privacy@classify.in and we will promptly delete the data.

For users between 13–18 years, we encourage parental guidance when using the platform and applying to coaching institutes.
