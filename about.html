💡 Our Mission

Making Coaching Discovery Transparent for Every Student

Every year, millions of Indian students and parents spend weeks — sometimes months — trying to figure out which coaching institute is right for JEE, NEET, NDA, CUET and other competitive exams.

They rely on word of mouth, random YouTube videos and outdated information. They visit multiple institutes, get confused by sales pitches, and often end up making the wrong choice — wasting lakhs of rupees and a full year.

Classify was built to fix this. We are India's first dedicated platform where students can discover, compare, and apply to coaching institutes — all in one place, with real verified data and honest student reviews.

📅 Our Journey

How Classify Started

From an idea to India's coaching discovery platform

2023 — The Problem
😤 We Struggled Too
Our founder spent 3 months trying to choose between coaching institutes for JEE. There was no reliable platform. Every source gave different information. The idea for Classify was born out of this frustration.
💡
🔨
2024 — Building
🚀 Started Building Classify
We started building the platform from scratch — designing the database structure, reaching out to institutes for data, and building the first version of the website with a small passionate team.
2024 — First Listings
🏫 First 50 Institutes Listed
We onboarded our first 50 coaching institutes across Kota, Delhi and Hyderabad. Students started discovering and comparing institutes on Classify for the first time.
🏫
📈
2025 — Growing
📈 500+ Institutes, 50K+ Students
Classify grew rapidly with 500+ institutes listed across 50 cities. Over 50,000 students used Classify to find and apply to coaching institutes. We expanded to cover JEE, NEET, NDA, CUET, UPSC and Banking exams.
2025 — Now
🎯 Building the Future
We're now building institute dashboards, AI-powered recommendations, and expanding to every city in India. Our goal — every student in India should be able to find their perfect coaching on Classify.

💰 Business Model

How Classify Works as a Business

Classify is completely free for students. Here's how we sustain ourselves.

🎯
Lead Commission
When a student applies to an institute through Classify, the institute pays us a small commission per verified lead. The institute gets a qualified student, the student gets into a great coaching — everyone wins.
⭐
Premium Listings
Institutes can pay for a featured/premium listing to appear higher in search results and get a verified badge. This is optional — basic listings are always free.
📊
Institute Dashboard
Institutes pay a monthly subscription for access to their dashboard — where they can see lead details, application analytics, and manage their Classify profile.
🤝
Always Free for Students
Browsing, comparing, reading reviews and applying through Classify is 100% free for students. No hidden fees, no subscriptions, no tricks. We make money from institutes, not students.
